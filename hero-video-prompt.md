# Hero Video Prompt for MyHorizon AI

## Video Specifications
- **Duration:** 15-20 second seamless loop
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (16:9)
- **Format:** MP4, H.264 codec
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Style:** Cinematic, futuristic, AI-driven transformations

## Core Prompt for Google Veo 4

```
Create a cinematic 15-second looping video showcasing AI transformation and automation. 

Visual Sequence:
1. Opening: Close-up of human hands typing on a keyboard, soft blue lighting
2. Transition: Hands dissolve into flowing digital particles and neural network patterns
3. Transformation: Morph traditional office scenes into futuristic holographic interfaces - similar to heads morphing into fishbowls but with business elements transforming into AI interfaces
4. Automation Display: Show abstract representations of:
   - Chat bubbles materializing and responding autonomously
   - Workflow diagrams lighting up and connecting automatically
   - Data streams flowing and organizing themselves
   - Robotic process automation arms elegantly handling digital documents
5. Climax: All elements converge into a central AI brain/neural network hub
6. Loop Return: Hub pulses and dissolves back to hands typing, completing the seamless loop

Color Palette:
- Primary: Deep space blues (#0F172A to #1E3A8A)
- Accents: Cyan electric (#06B6D4), purple energy (#8B5CF6)
- Highlights: White/silver digital particles
- Gradients: Blue-to-purple transitions throughout

Camera Movement:
- Smooth, floating dolly movements
- Slow-motion emphasis on transformation moments
- Depth of field shifts to guide focus
- 360° orbit around central AI hub during climax

Technical Effects:
- Volumetric lighting with god rays through digital particles
- Holographic UI elements with scan-line effects
- Neural network connections pulsing with energy
- Liquid-like transitions between scenes (similar to fish bowl morphing effect)
- Subtle chromatic aberration for futuristic feel

Mood & Tone:
- Sophisticated and premium
- Futuristic but not dystopian
- Elegant automation, not cold robotics
- Inspiring transformation, not threatening replacement

No Text/Branding: Video should be abstract enough to stand on its own without text overlays (text will be added in post-production)

Style References:
- Apple product launch video aesthetics
- Sci-fi UI from "Minority Report" or "Iron Man"
- Abstract tech showcase videos from IBM Watson or Google AI
- The morphing transformation style you mentioned (heads to fishbowls) but applied to business/tech elements
```
